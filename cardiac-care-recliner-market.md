<p><p>The cardiac care recliner market includes two main types: manual and automatic recliners. Manual recliners rely on a simple lever mechanism, allowing caregivers to adjust the position of the chair based on patient needs. These are typically more cost-effective and easy to maintain. In contrast, automatic recliners utilize electric mechanisms for adjustments, offering greater convenience and comfort with features like multiple positioning settings and mobility aids. Both types are designed to enhance patient comfort during cardiac care procedures.</p></p>
