<p><p>The structural fumigation service market is segmented into three types based on the state of the fumigant used: solid, liquid, and gas. Solid fumigation utilizes solid formulations that release gas when heated or mixed with water, effective for targeting pests in confined spaces. Liquid fumigants are applied as sprays or drenches, providing deeper penetration into materials. Gas fumigation involves introducing gaseous chemicals into an enclosed environment, ensuring comprehensive pest control over large areas, particularly for structures and stored goods.</p></p>
