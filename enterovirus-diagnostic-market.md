<p><p>The Enterovirus Diagnostic Market encompasses three primary testing types: </p><p>1. **Viral Isolation Testing** involves culturing the virus from patient samples, allowing for direct identification and study of the virus.  </p><p>2. **Serology Testing** detects antibodies in the blood, indicating past or current infections based on the immune response.  </p><p>3. **Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) Assay** is a molecular technique that amplifies viral genetic material, providing rapid and precise identification of enteroviruses. Each method serves distinct diagnostic purposes in clinical settings.</p></p>
