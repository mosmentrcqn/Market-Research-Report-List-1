<p><strong>The X-Ray Bone Densitometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peripheral Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry</li><li>Central Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptionmetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Bone Densitometer market includes two primary types: Peripheral Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDXA) and Central Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (cDXA). pDXA is typically used for screening and provides measurements at peripheral sites like the wrist or heel, making it portable and suitable for primary care settings. In contrast, cDXA measures bone density at central skeletal sites such as the spine and hip, offering comprehensive evaluations for diagnosing osteoporosis and assessing fracture risk.</p></p>
