<p><p>Polyisobutylene (PIB) is categorized into three market types based on molecular weight: low, medium, and high. Low molecular weight PIB is primarily used in adhesives and sealants due to its tackiness and flexibility. Medium molecular weight PIB serves as an additive in lubricants and fuel, enhancing performance. High molecular weight PIB is utilized in applications requiring durability, such as rubber products and asphalt modifiers. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, influencing production processes and end-user applications.</p></p>
