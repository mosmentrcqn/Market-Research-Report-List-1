<p><strong>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically Steered</li><li>Mechanically Steered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas market is divided into two main types: electronically steered and mechanically steered antennas. Electronically steered antennas use phased array technology to adjust their beam direction quickly and accurately without moving parts, offering enhanced performance for dynamic applications like satellite communications. In contrast, mechanically steered antennas rely on physical movement to change their orientation, providing reliable performance but typically with slower response times. Each type caters to specific needs in telecommunications, broadcasting, and aviation sectors.</p></p>
