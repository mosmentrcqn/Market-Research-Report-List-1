<p><p>Electrostatic Oil Cleaners (EOCs) are advanced filtration systems used to remove contaminants from oils through electrostatic charges. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Phase and Three Phase Electrostatic Oil Cleaners. Single Phase EOCs are designed for simpler, lower-capacity applications, efficiently removing particulates in single-phase oil. In contrast, Three Phase EOCs cater to more complex systems, handling higher capacities and effectively separating contaminants from multi-phase fluids, making them suitable for industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
